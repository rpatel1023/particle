Immortal Bot

  • An Esports Match Tracking Discord Bot

Immortal Bot is a match-scraping Discord Bot designed to notify users about upcoming professional matches in various esports, such as Dota 2, League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and more! ImmortalBot is build on the discord.py library and is used by various esport organizations and professionals!
